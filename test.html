Project Overview: Semenyih District

  • The project is located in the Semenyih district of Selangor, Malaysia. The population in the area is estimated around 95,000 people. Mostly is Chinese and Malays.
  • It is about 10KM from the well-established Kajang Town (Population is about 400,000 people).
  • The existing accessibility is nicely done up. It is just a 30KM drive to Kuala Lumpur City Center (KLCC).
  • A low density town that has attracted crowds from the group of first home buyers and investors to come and buy houses in Semenyih.
  • It has a nice natural green environment and excellent ecological conditions.
  • Complete education system, from kid learning schools to primary schools to universities/colleges. From local schools to international schools, all within the project.
  • A lot of well-known developers have moved to develop in the KL southern corridor. The potential for the area to become a hotspot is just a few years down the road.
